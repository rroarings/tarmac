Learn what we aim to provide at Trackside

Trackside is an unofficial Formula 1 fan website created and operated by a solo developer and passionate F1 enthusiast. As an independent one-person operation, Trackside aims to deliver engaging content that celebrates the sport, its teams, drivers, and thrilling moments on and off the track.

Focal points of Trackside

Digestable race season breakdowns

Explore concise yet informative recaps of each F1 season, from dramatic race results to pivotal moments. These breakdowns are designed to give you a quick understanding of the season's story without overwhelming you with details.

Season Highlights

Explore detailed breakdowns of the 2023, 2024, and 2025 F1 seasons, including race results, standings, and memorable moments.

Driver Stats

Dive into general statistics for your favorite drivers, including performance insights and race-by-race summaries.

Race Information

Get an in-depth look at individual races, with recreated moments captured using F1 23 and F1 24 video games.

Original Footage
