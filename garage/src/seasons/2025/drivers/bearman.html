Oliver Bearman, born on May 8, 2005, in Chelmsford, England, makes his full-time Formula 1 debut in 2025 with Haas, bringing youthful energy and raw speed to a team eager to climb the competitive ladder. After impressing in junior categories and turning heads with his composed stand-in performance for Ferrari in 2024, Bearman earned his shot at the big time. Now paired with seasoned campaigner Esteban Ocon, Bearman enters the sport ready to learn, compete, and grow as one of the most exciting British prospects in recent memory.

Bearman’s early races in 2025 have already showcased the hallmarks of a future star: confident wheel-to-wheel racing, strong adaptation to new circuits, and clear communication with the garage. His aggressive but controlled style makes him a natural fit for Haas’ current mindset—hungry, unafraid, and determined to take the fight to the established midfield teams. With Ocon as a benchmark, Bearman has a unique opportunity to fast-track his learning curve in an environment that values both development and ambition.

For Bearman, the 2025 season is all about proving he belongs at the highest level. Whether scoring his first points, mastering challenging conditions, or making a statement in qualifying, every race is a new chance to build momentum. If his trajectory continues on its current path, Oliver Bearman could very well become a pillar of British motorsport in the years to come.
