Esteban Ocon, born on September 17, 1996, in Évreux, France, embarks on a bold new chapter in 2025 as he joins Haas following a multi-season tenure at Alpine. Ocon’s move reflects both a desire for a fresh start and an opportunity to take on a leadership role within an evolving team. Known for his gritty racecraft, strong race-day execution, and unshakable focus, Ocon brings valuable experience and a tenacious spirit to Haas as they aim to rise from the back of the grid into a consistent midfield presence.

Now partnered with highly rated British rookie Oliver Bearman, Ocon is stepping into a mentorship role while continuing to pursue his own ambitions in the sport. His ability to dig deep in challenging situations—often pulling off strong results in difficult cars—makes him an ideal fit for a Haas squad undergoing significant restructuring. With new investment and technical partnerships improving the team’s outlook, Ocon is tasked with guiding development and extracting every ounce of performance from the VF-25.

For Ocon, 2025 is as much about reinvention as it is resurgence. No longer in the shadows of larger factory operations, he now has the platform to lead, influence, and potentially surprise. If Haas can provide him with the machinery, Ocon has proven time and again that he can deliver—whether it’s fighting in the points, fending off faster cars, or capitalizing on chaos. This season is a critical opportunity to redefine his trajectory in the ever-competitive world of Formula 1.
