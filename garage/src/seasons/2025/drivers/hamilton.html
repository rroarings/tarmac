Lewis Hamilton, born on January 7, 1985, in Stevenage, England, remains one of Formula 1’s most iconic figures, even in his 19th full season on the grid. After an illustrious career with Mercedes that included six World Championships, Hamilton made the bold and historic switch to Ferrari for the 2025 season, a move that stunned the motorsport world and fulfilled a lifelong dream. Partnering with Charles Leclerc, Hamilton now finds himself at the heart of Ferrari’s push for a long-awaited drivers’ title.

The move to Maranello marks a new chapter for Hamilton—one driven not by legacy, but by passion. His wealth of experience, tireless work ethic, and unmatched ability to build a team around him have already begun to influence Ferrari's approach. While adapting to a new environment after over a decade at Mercedes presents its challenges, Hamilton has shown renewed energy and focus, committed to pushing both himself and Ferrari to the top of the podium once more.

Hamilton's presence at Ferrari has brought both gravity and belief to the Scuderia’s championship ambitions. He’s not just there to mentor—he’s there to win. As the 2025 season unfolds, Hamilton continues to chase an unprecedented eighth World Championship, determined to add one final, dramatic flourish to an already legendary career. Whether or not that crown comes, his decision to don red has already added a bold and captivating new twist to his storied journey.
