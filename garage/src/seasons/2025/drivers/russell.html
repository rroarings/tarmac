George Russell, born on February 15, 1998, in King’s Lynn, England, has become the face of Mercedes’ new era in Formula 1. After joining the Silver Arrows full-time in 2022, Russell patiently worked alongside seven-time World Champion Lewis Hamilton, learning and evolving into a consistent front-runner. Known for his precision, professionalism, and sharp qualifying pace, Russell became a race winner in 2022 and has since grown into a reliable team leader as Mercedes navigates the changing competitive landscape of the sport.

Now in 2025, Russell leads the Mercedes team alongside highly anticipated rookie Kimi Antonelli, who steps into the legendary shoes of Hamilton. As the senior figure within the team, Russell plays a dual role: pushing for podiums and wins while also guiding Antonelli through his debut season. While Mercedes hasn't yet returned to its dominant pre-2021 form, Russell has remained a constant force, maximizing every opportunity and helping steer the team's technical direction through evolving regulations.

With several years of top-tier experience and an unwavering belief in the Mercedes project, Russell remains hungry for his first World Championship. He’s earned a reputation for his race intelligence, calm under pressure, and willingness to fight hard against the likes of Verstappen, Leclerc, and Norris. As Mercedes looks to reassert itself as a title contender, Russell’s leadership and performances in 2025 are central to the team's hopes of reclaiming their place at the top of the sport.
