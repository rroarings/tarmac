Pulse Pounding 2024 Moments

May 21, 2025 by Nick

The 2024 FIA Formula One World Championship delivered 24 races packed with drama, speed, and unforgettable thrills. From Lando Norris chasing Max Verstappen to the wire in Imola to McLaren’s triumphant Constructors’ title in Abu Dhabi, relive the pulse-pounding moments that had fans on the edge of their seats.

Key Moments from the 2024 Season

A Rollercoaster of Speed and Drama

The 2024 FIA Formula One World Championship was a record-breaking season with 24 Grands Prix, six sprint races, and countless moments that left fans breathless. Max Verstappen clinched his fourth consecutive Drivers’ Championship in Las Vegas, but McLaren stole the spotlight by ending Red Bull’s reign with their first Constructors’ title since 1998 at Abu Dhabi. From nail-biting overtakes to shocking disqualifications, here are five pulse-pounding moments that defined 2024’s thrilling F1 season.

Norris’s First Win in Miami

The Miami Grand Prix was a turning point for McLaren and Lando Norris, who secured his maiden F1 win on May 5, 2024. After a safety car reshuffled the pack, Norris capitalized on his upgraded MCL38, holding off a charging Max Verstappen to take the checkered flag. The crowd erupted as Norris’s radio crackled with joy, marking a pivotal moment in his battle to pressure Verstappen’s championship lead. This win showcased McLaren’s resurgence, setting the stage for their Constructors’ title run.

Imola’s Gripping Finale

The Emilia Romagna Grand Prix on May 19, 2024, delivered one of the season’s most intense finishes. Lando Norris, in his McLaren, hunted down Max Verstappen’s Red Bull in the final laps, closing the gap to just 0.725 seconds at the finish line. Fans held their breath as Norris pushed his MCL38 to the limit, but Verstappen’s masterful defense secured the win. This near-miss underscored the tightening competition, with McLaren emerging as a serious threat.

Hungary’s McLaren Radio Drama

The Hungarian Grand Prix on July 21, 2024, was a masterclass in drama. McLaren’s Lando Norris and Oscar Piastri battled for the lead, but team orders sparked controversy. Norris, initially ahead, was asked to let Piastri pass for his first win, leading to tense radio exchanges that gripped fans. Meanwhile, Max Verstappen’s collision with Lewis Hamilton added fuel to the chaos, with Verstappen’s frustration boiling over. This race showcased McLaren’s internal dynamics and the season’s heated rivalries.

Russell’s Shock Disqualification in Spa

The Belgian Grand Prix on July 28, 2024, delivered a gut-punch for Mercedes and George Russell. Russell executed a bold one-stop strategy to claim victory, crossing the line ahead of teammate Lewis Hamilton. But post-race inspections revealed his car was underweight, leading to a disqualification that handed Hamilton the win. The dramatic twist stunned fans and highlighted the fine margins in F1, with Mercedes’ “randomly super fast” pace stealing headlines.

Ferrari’s Civil War in Las Vegas

The Las Vegas Grand Prix on November 23, 2024, saw Ferrari’s Charles Leclerc and Carlos Sainz Jr. battling fiercely, sparking a “civil war” on the track. As Verstappen clinched his fourth Drivers’ Championship, Ferrari’s internal rivalry stole the show, with Leclerc and Sainz pushing each other to the limit in a high-stakes duel for podium positions. The intensity foreshadowed the 2025 season, with Hamilton set to join Leclerc at Ferrari. This moment captured Ferrari’s passion and competitive fire.

Relive the 2024 Thrills

The 2024 F1 season was a wild ride of first wins, epic chases, team dramas, and shocking twists. From Norris’s Miami triumph to Ferrari’s Las Vegas showdown, these moments defined a year of relentless competition. Want more?
